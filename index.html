Our Story

❮ ❯

It all began with a simple message on a dating site two strangers brought together by chance, curiosity, and maybe a little bit of fate.

We didn't know it then, but that first conversation would grow into something truly life-changing. What started as late-night chats and shared laughter turned into weekend dates, road trips, quiet moments, and the kind of love that feels like home.

Over the past four years, we’ve built a life together one filled with love, growth, understanding, and joy. We’ve cheered each other on, held each other through challenges, and continued to choose one another every single day.

Now, we’re ready for our next chapter not just as partners, but as husband and wife. We can’t wait to celebrate this moment with you, the people who’ve been part of our journey.

With all our love,
Lester & Tin
